How it works

Style & Purpose Determination

These include determining the purpose and function of the space, defining the style and theme

Design Development

Choosing a color palette, selecting appropriate furniture, lighting, and accessories, creating a layout or floor plan, and finally, selecting materials and finishes, and costing.

Final Step

Gathering all documents related to the project, shopping list, adding finishing touches such as artwork and decor.and conducting a final walk-through with the client to ensure their satisfaction. This comprehensive approach ensures that every detail is addressed and the project is completed to the highest standards.

Supervision

Step-by-step supervision by an interior designer is crucial for ensuring a successful project. Firstly, the designer will conduct a comprehensive assessment of the space requirements and the client's preferences. Next, they will present design concepts and collaborate with the client to fine-tune a final design. Once the final design is approved, the interior designer will oversee the project's execution to ensure the quality of work delivered. This involves coordinating with contractors, craftsmen, and other relevant parties, frequent site visits, and on-site problem-solving. Finally, the designer will provide finishing touches and styling advice to complete the project.
